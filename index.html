Check My Deficit
Reads Apple Health + Garmin TDEE → shows deficit zone
1

Find Health Samples

Type: Dietary Energy → Date: Today

Variable: TodayCalories
2

Calculate Statistics

Input: TodayCalories → Statistic: Sum

Variable: TotalEaten
3

Ask for Input — TDEE

Prompt: "Enter Garmin TDEE for today"
Variable: TDEE
4

Calculate Deficit

TDEE − TotalEaten = Deficit
5

If/Otherwise Conditions

If Deficit > 1000 → "⚠️ Too aggressive!"

If Deficit 500–1000 → "✅ Perfect zone!"

If Deficit 0–500 → "⚡ Too small"

Otherwise → "🚨 Eating more than burning!"
6

Show Summary Alert

TDEE: [TDEE] | Eaten: [TotalEaten] | Deficit: [Deficit]
💡 Best time to run: Before dinner — know exactly how much to eat tonight to stay in the 500–750 deficit zone.
